An essay (/ˈɛs.eɪ/, ESS-ay) is, generally, a piece of writing that gives the author's own argument,
but the definition is vague, overlapping with those of a letter, a paper, an article, a pamphlet,
and a short story. Essays have been sub-classified as formal and informal: formal essays are characterized by "serious purpose, dignity,
logical organization, length," whereas the informal essay is characterized by "the personal element (self-revelation, individual tastes.
